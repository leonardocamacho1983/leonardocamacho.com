# Localization Glossary

## Canonical Terms
- Compounding Advantage
- Organizational Design
- Strategic Clarity
- Leadership Cadence
- Operating System (OS)

## Localization Rules
- Preserve strategic meaning over literal translation.
- Keep product/program names in canonical form unless locale policy says otherwise.
- Keep internal taxonomy consistent across locales (essay, insight, research, note).
- Preserve tone: authoritative, concrete, non-hype.

## QA Checklist
- Terminology consistency
- Cultural and idiomatic correctness
- No anglicism drift when unnecessary
- Claims and nuance preserved from source text
