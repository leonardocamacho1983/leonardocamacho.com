# Newsletter Playbook

## Objective
- Convert strategic essays into high-intent newsletter distribution without diluting quality.

## Message Priorities
- Lead with one strategic tension.
- Make the reader outcome explicit in 1 sentence.
- Include one concrete reason to read now.

## Subject/Preview Rules
- Subject line: clear, specific, no clickbait.
- Preview text: extends subject with context, not repetition.
- Keep positioning aligned with brand voice and editorial standards.

## CTA Rules
- Single primary CTA.
- Action-oriented and context-aware.
- Avoid manipulative urgency.
